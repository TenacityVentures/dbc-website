About the Organization

Empowering Families, Protecting Children

"DBC is a legally constituted, non-political, and non-sectarian non-profit organization operating in Sierra Leone since 2022."

Guided by our Constitution and aligned with internationally accepted best practices, we believe children thrive best when families are strengthened and communities are supported to care for them.

Our Vision

A Sierra Leone where every child has the opportunity to thrive, learn, and reach their full potential.

Our Mission

To improve lives through education support, child protection, and economic empowerment.

Our Core Values

  • Child-centred Care
  • Accountability
  • Community Participation
  • Sustainability

How We Help

Focused strategies for long-term community transformation.

Education

Providing materials and mentorship to ensure success in school for vulnerable children.

Child Protection

Working with communities to prevent abuse and promote family-based care.

Health

Supporting psychosocial well-being and nutrition for healthy child development.

Empowerment

Skills training and livelihoods for families to reach economic self-reliance.
